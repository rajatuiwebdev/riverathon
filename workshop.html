About the Workshop

LiDAR technology is transforming river and riparian monitoring by providing detailed data for studying morphology, erosion hotspots, vegetation analysis, and floodplain dynamics. It aids in disaster preparedness, predictive modeling, digital twin development, and sustainable water resource management.

This workshop delves into LiDAR’s practical applications for solving real-world river challenges, offering insights into environmental and climate management solutions.

Schedule

Day 1: LiDAR & River Engineering

LiDAR Basics:
Principles, System types (Airborne, Terrestrial, Mobile, Bathymetric), data acquisition, and processing.

River Engineering:
Fundamentals, challenges, and LiDAR applications for real-time solutions.

Day 2: LiDAR workflow & Integration

LiDAR Processing:
Tools, point cloud filtering (ground/non-ground), and models (DTM, DSM, CHM).

Integration:
LiDAR with drones, GIS, AI; use cases in hydrology, vegetation, infrastructure, and climate monitoring.
